About AliVet

The AliVet Veterinary Clinic offer an extensive range of Professional Veterinary Services, Animal Care Products and Boarding Facilities. Our qualified staff are passionate about animal health and continually complete further training and development to increase their knowledge and remain up to date with current products and procedures. We promote best practice for the pets we care for, as they are part of the AliVet Family. Through our many wellness programs and services by “putting your pet’s paws in our hands” they may live happy and healthy lives..
